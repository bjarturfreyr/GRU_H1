Guesthouse Sólbær is located in a quiet street, a 2 minute walk from the see front. It is a small guesthouse with a welcoming and friendly atmosphere. All of our rooms are with shared bathrooms, one on each floor. To make the guest comfortable there is a sitting lounge where guests can sit and relax, drink coffee and plan the next steps of the trip, or enjoy the garden view. We offer good kitchen facilities with for examble micro oven. On the Terrace there is a barbiqe (grill)Wifi is available in all areas and is free of charge Free private parking is possible on site
Guesthouse Sólbær is a small “Bed & Breakfast “,with friendly and warm atmosphere. The small size of our bed & breakfast brings relaxed energy to the house and our guests. Our sitting lounge is the heart of the house with the nice terrace to enjoy the garden view. In the garden is a good playground for children, with trampoline and swings. We try our very best to fulfil our guests wishes and assist them in finding an activity and restaurant in the area or anything else. Our breakfast consists of Icelandic traditional products with twist of laval products.
To make the guest comfortable there is a sitting lounge where guests can sit and relax, drink coffee and plan the next steps of the trip, or enjoy the garden view. The terrace with sitting area and the hot tube / whirlpool is always a big hit with our guests. Our information corner has brochures and information on the activities and restaurants in the local area. We are happy to assist our guests in their planning and reservation for tours etc. For our tests who do not travel with their computer or iPad, there is a computer corner with computer where they can use our computer and Skype home
